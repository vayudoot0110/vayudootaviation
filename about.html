About Us

Vayudoot Aviation

Story

VAYUDOOT Aviation is an organization put together by highly Qualified & experienced professionals within the aviation industry. Individuals who are highly experienced pilots, cabin crew, flight dispatchers are all part of the team at VAYUDOOT Aviation. They have all attained the standard of excellence required in their field, We passionate about the success of our students. VAYUDOOT Aviation knows the aviation industry thoroughly, the training and professionalism necessary to succeed.
We are connected with the best flight schools in the world, which ensures their students become the world’s best pilots, with amazingly successful careers. VAYUDOOT Aviation also recognizes the importance of non-flying careers in the industry, and trains the best Assistant flight dispatchers, cabin crew in the world. The aviation industry demands the best; that’s why We always train best…..!
VAYUDOOT Aviation has locations in Mumbai & New Delhi and also the RTR(A) Sessions goes through all over the INDIA periodically. VAYUDOOT Aviation has seen rapid growth due to the excellence in training, and its outstanding track record of building careers in aviation. VAYUDOOT Aviation is there to Fulfil the growing demands of the aviation industry in every way. The enthusiasm of its management and staff are the reason the students who train with VAYUDOOT Aviation are so passionate and successful.

VAYUDOOT Aviation stands behind all students at every step of their career. From assisting the students pick the course that’s right for them, to the training, to interview preparation, to job placement assistance; VAYUDOOT Aviation does whatever it takes to ensure success of the students.VAYUDOOT Aviation is committed to the success of every student. Students who want a great career and life-style train with VAYUDOOT Aviation
VAYUDOOT Aviation is not only concerned about the student’s academic and career success, it is also concerned with the emotional well-being and ensuring the students has the best environment for training. This includes recreational activities, such as dinners, and trips to popular attractions at the location of training.

Our

VISION

We at VAYUDOOT Aviation aim to be the best aviation training organisation. We assure comprehensive training and keep continuous watch to improve results periodically. To assist the students in all the aspects. To make all necessary preparations for studying in another country. To provide the best training facilities on the most modern aircraft. To fully prepare the student for the selection process of the airlines including: preparation for the aptitude, psychometric, interview, and simulator evaluations To provide full job placement assistance to ensure student has a successful career in aviation. To supply the industry with well trained, competent pilots to meet the demands of the industry.

Our

Mission

To turn aspiring pilots into world-class pilots by providing them with schools who provide the best Pilot training, and to assist the student in every way of achieving a successful career in futurre in which they will make a significant contribution to the Aviation industry.

Our

MOTTO

We work with our trainees from day one, ensuring that their training is done with the highest safety precautions. Also we ensure Building the best is our motto and that is why we adapt our courses to meet the needs of each individual applicant. We believe in transparent relation, building the strong base of the student career for future.
